FAQ

A summary of frequently asked questions.

About me?

With 5 years of in-house experience in partnerships and affiliate marketing, I’ve delivered significant growth across multiple programs at companies including Eneba, Holafly, and GigSky. I’ve worked with a wide range of publishers — from cashback and coupon sites to influencers, bloggers, price comparison platforms, loyalty programs, media partners, and niche content creators. I specialize in scaling revenue through strategic affiliate initiatives and securing high-value partnerships that deliver measurable results, all while building strong, mutually beneficial relationships that drive long-term success..

What stack do you prefer?

Whatever integrates cleanly: partner network or in-house tracking, server-side events where possible, and clear attribution logic. We’ll pick based on your economics and stage.

How do you measure incrementality?

Pre/post tests, holdouts where workable, and contribution margin by cohort — not just last-click. If we can’t prove value, we won’t scale it.

How fast can we start?

Blueprint within two weeks; recruiting begins as soon as tracking is reliable and payouts are signed off.

Do you work on rev-share?

Base + performance is possible once measurement is trustworthy. Pure rev-share without visibility usually isn’t a fit.
